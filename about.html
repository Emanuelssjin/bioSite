About Ethan

Meet Ethan, a curious and cheerful 5-year-old who just started kindergarten. With eyes that show he is always motivated to take on the world, he's always ready for his next adventure. He carries an insatiable fascination for trains, from freight trains to the historic steam locomotives. Ethan's world is filled with stories of trains and different types of carriers. His excitement knows no bounds as he eagerly waits to share his train tales with his new kindergarten friends.
